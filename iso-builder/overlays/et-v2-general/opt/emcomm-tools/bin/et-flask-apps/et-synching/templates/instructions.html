Bluetooth GPS Sync

Sync time and grid location from an Android device or Kenwood HT
Two GPS sources are supported: the LiaisonGPS Android app, or a Kenwood TH-D74 / TH-D75 handheld radio with built-in GPS. Follow the steps for your device below.
1

Android: Install the LiaisonGPS app

Download and install the LiaisonGPS APK directly: LiaisonGPS.apk. The app broadcasts your GPS position over Bluetooth as NMEA sentences.

Kenwood TH-D74 / TH-D75: No app needed. Enable GPS on the radio and make sure it has a fix before continuing.

2

Pair your device via Bluetooth

If not already paired, go to Settings → Bluetooth on this computer and complete pairing with your Android phone or Kenwood HT. The device must appear as paired before continuing.

3

Android: Start LiaisonGPS and wait for a GPS fix

Open the LiaisonGPS app. When prompted, allow GPS access. Wait until latitude and longitude coordinates are displayed on screen — this confirms your phone has a GPS fix.

Kenwood HT: Confirm the GPS icon is active and coordinates are visible on the radio display.

4

Android: Keep LiaisonGPS open and in the foreground

The app must remain active during the connection. Do not lock the screen or switch to another app until the sync is complete.

Kenwood HT: No action needed — the radio streams GPS automatically over Bluetooth.
